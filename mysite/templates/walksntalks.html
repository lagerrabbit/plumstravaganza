In the Footsteps of George Duckworth
Plumstead Station
11am - 1pm 03 Sep
A walk and talk around parts of Plumstead that have changed over the years, pointing out sites that where important in the past and buildings, whose purpose has changed. The walk will follow the route that the social commentator, George Duckworth, took along with PC Clyne on Monday 1st October 1900, as part of the Social Survey of London Life that was carried out between 1886 and 1903. This survey was instigated by the businessman, Charles Booth, and it is hoped that the walk will shed some light on what life was like in parts of Plumstead 116 years ago. Maybe even in the very house that you live in today.
