Plumstravaganza 2016 – Celebrating Plumstead SE18

Community, Festival and Fun

Art Plumstead is a community group that has been putting on events in Plumstead since 2012. At a meeting of the group in the autumn of 2015 a casual mention of the Dragon Trail held in Norwich that summer led on to the idea that Plumstead could do something similar and call it the Plum Trail and with it the idea of Plumstravaganza 2016 was born, because many good things are on offer in Plumstead that many people don’t know about.

So what is the Plumstravaganza?

  • A calendar of events over a two week period at the time when summer shifts into autumn 29th August – September 11th 2016
  • Created around the Plum Trail, to showcase clubs, businesses and other amenities that offer something positive to our community. Some of the events occur all year round, others have been tailor made for Plumstravaganza
  • An enjoyable festival of mostly free activities for people to join in.
We are delighted that Plumstead Make Merry and the Plumstead Community Market are taking part in Plumstravaganza, as they have given so much to Plumstead over the years. We’re a voluntary group with no outside funding and we hope to bring a sense of festival and fun to the streets and hope that as many people as possible get involved in what Plumstead has to offer!
