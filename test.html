PLUMSTRAVAGANZA

29th August to 11th September 2016

About

Plumstravaganza 2016 is about recognising everything Plumstead has to offer and celebrating the contribution made by individuals, local groups and small businesses to the community. It is about bringing a sense of festival and fun to the streets.

Events

We have had the following ideas suggested at our open meetings:

Plum Trail, Poetry Competition, Short Story Competition, Open Gardens, Open Studios, Fun Plum Run, Foraging – Possibly linked to Cooking afterwards, Guerilla Art/Pavement Chalking, Poppy Making W/Shop, Community Felt Making, Come And Sing, For People Who Can Read A Musical Score, Musical Recital, Plum Quiz Night, Open Air (Or Inside) Painting/Drawing, Jam Competition, Tree Trail, Making Workshop From Natural Materials, Final Party, Pub Crawl, History Walk.
